What you're looking at, in plain English

These 93 events are real Polymarket markets that have already ended in the last four months. For each one we know who won and what the prices were at the end. The page pretends you ran a trading strategy on every single one of these, then tallies up how much money you would have made or lost.

The "trade at prices from" selector above is the important knob. Prices at the moment a market closes are almost always correct (because by then everyone knows the answer). Prices a day before the market closes are less tight — people make more mistakes, and that's where real profit lives. Try switching between the three options and watch the numbers change.

Fair warning: the “24h before close” and “7d before close” prices are estimates — we work them out from how much each outcome's price moved in that window. A few trades with huge gaps are probably wrong (they come from outcomes that didn't exist a week ago). But most of the data is real enough to trust the pattern: the earlier you look, the more genuine arbitrage you find.
