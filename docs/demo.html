how this works

This page opens a WebSocket directly from your browser to Polymarket's public CLOB (ws-subscriptions-clob.polymarket.com), subscribes to a handful of active negRisk categorical events, maintains L2 order-book state per outcome token, and recomputes the basket-arb math on every delta. It's a port of the core engine from the Python repo. No server, no backend — just your browser.

When Σ best-ask across all outcomes of an event drops below $1.00, that event card turns green and lands in the opportunities stream on the right.
